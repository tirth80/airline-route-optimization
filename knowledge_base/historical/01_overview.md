# Flight Delay Analysis - Overview

## Dataset Summary

This analysis is based on **4,542,343 US domestic flights** from 2019.

## Key Statistics

- **Total Flights Analyzed**: 4,542,343
- **Overall Delay Rate**: 18.9%
- **Total Delayed Flights**: 859,158
- **Average Delay Duration**: 57 minutes (when delayed)

## Key Findings

### Best Times to Fly
- **Best Month**: September (14% delay rate)
- **Best Day**: Saturday (15% delay rate)
- **Best Time**: Early morning 5-7 AM (12% delay rate)

### Worst Times to Fly
- **Worst Month**: June (24% delay rate)
- **Worst Day**: Thursday (22% delay rate)
- **Worst Time**: Evening 6-9 PM (28% delay rate)

## Model Performance

The LightGBM prediction model achieves:
- **Accuracy**: 82.67%
- **ROC-AUC**: 0.7496
- **Precision**: 79.3%
- **Recall**: 74.1%

## Total Cost Impact

Annual delay costs at major hubs exceed **$47 million**, including:
- Passenger compensation
- Crew overtime
- Fuel costs
- Airport fees
