# Flight Delay Cost Analysis

## Cost Breakdown Per Delay

### Passenger Costs
- **Per passenger per hour**: $47
- **Meal vouchers**: $15-25
- **Hotel accommodation**: $150-200 (overnight delays)
- **Rebooking fees absorbed**: $75-200

### Airline Operational Costs
- **Crew overtime per hour**: $250-500
- **Fuel for idling aircraft**: $50-100/hour
- **Gate fees extended**: $100-300/hour
- **Maintenance scheduling**: $200-400

### Airport Costs
- **Gate reassignment**: $150-250
- **Ground crew overtime**: $100-200/hour
- **Customer service staff**: $75-150/hour
