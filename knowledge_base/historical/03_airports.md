## Regional Patterns

### East Coast Airports
Higher delays during winter months (Dec-Feb) due to snow and ice.

### West Coast Airports
Higher delays during summer due to fog (especially SFO).

### Midwest Airports
Seasonal variation with thunderstorms in summer affecting ORD heavily.
